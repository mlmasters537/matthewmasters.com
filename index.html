Who Am I

Friendly Neighborhood Creative

My name is Matthew Masters. I am a freelance graphic designer based in beautiful Austin, Texas. I enjoy being at what I consider the nexus of design. Designs power doesn’t come from its images or the artwork, neither is it the copy or the typography. It's when all these parts come together and harmonizes into something that excites and entices as well as inform. It is the invisible system that links all these pieces that gives it power, memorability and mass appeal.

“I believe that there are no absolutes, maxims or sacred cows in a creative process. It is my core principle that no decision is ever truly wrong in design. That, no matter the predominant rule or its acceptance by the majority, there is always the exception. Those who embrace the exception will always find something new, as you can’t blaze new trails walking down the same path everyone else did.” - mm
