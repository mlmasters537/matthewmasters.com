Experiments in Typography

Post, or PO5T, was a passion project that came about from my increasingly curious addiction to Typography, particulalry more expressive post-modernist designs. I drew inspiration from legendary titans like Dave Carson, Paula Scher, and wolfgang weingart.

The idea behind the project was to visualize my interpretation of a series of 90’s songs. The project had two rules. Rule one, it should not like look like previous work or use imagery from it. Rule two was that the design had to be something that a younger version of me, jumping around with my guitar on full blast, would put on my bedroom wall.

Post, also gave me the opportunity to experiment with typeface I didn’t normally use at the time, like DIN next, Bodoni, Aviner, etc.
