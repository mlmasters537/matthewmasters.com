The Process

A New Logo to Move Forward

In researching wines for the project, I found that different wine profiles are naturally achieved simply by the vineyards geological placement and that a good wines flavor should reflect its location. This gave me to idea that drinking wine is a journey - a moment - when you are taken to another place. I designed the label to reflect this idea of a journey by symbolically tying it to an image of a hot air balloon ride slowly drifting over a vineyard on a perfect summer’s sunset. The vine displays Pinot Gris grapes and a flower blossoms to identify the floral tones specifically found in the Canvas wines Pinot Gorgio flavor profile. I was very proud that is piece achieved second place in this national contest.
