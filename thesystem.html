The Challenge

Stronger Identity with a Better System

I had become fascinated with U.S. surveillance and capabilities after watching Laura Poitras’s 2014 documentary Citizen Four. The film followed the story of Edward Snowden soon after he had copied and leaked classified information from the National Security Agency (NSA) in 2013 without authorization. The System (or TH3 SY5T3M) was designed to be open ended and visually schizophrenic using Post-Modern ideals. The book itself is hand-bound and incorporates experimental print idea using transparent + layering.

Editoral Layout
Refreshing a Classic

Layout and art direction direction includes a mash-up of traditional 90’s inspired Grunge with more modern Glitch effects and stylings. The core concept was was to design in a visually schizopheric manner. A core inspriartion was the title credits to the film Seven where John Doe’s is creating his notebooks.

Photography
More Functionality

The cover incorporates my personal photography taken from an old passion project utilizing only photography taken from mobile devices. This shot in particular is a macro shot of a hundred dollar bill, visually giving the reader the impression of Benjamin Franklin (a symbol of the US government) is peeking in on the viewer.

Binding + Effects
Better Delivery

The System was designed, first and foremost, with the print medium in mind. An integral part of the project was to incorporate transparent pages that interacted with the pages below it. The binding of the book, a sandwich style, was inspired by Massimo’s The Vignelli Canon.
